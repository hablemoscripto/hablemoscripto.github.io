# Discord Community Structure — Hablemos Cripto

**Status:** Internal guidelines (May 2026)

## Core Principle
The Discord is a **paid benefit**, not a free community. This protects the perceived value of the Intermedio and Fundador/Experto plans.

Free users should **not** have access to the Discord server.

## Roles

- **Intermedio**: Full platform access only
- **Fundador**: Permanent role for the first 100 buyers of the top tier
- **Experto**: Role for everyone who purchases the top tier after the 100 Fundador spots are sold
- **Fundador (legacy)**: Special permanent badge/role for the original 100

## Recommended Channel Structure

### Visible to all paid members (Intermedio + Fundador/Experto)
- #bienvenida
- #anuncios
- #general
- #preguntas-y-discusion
- #soporte-plataforma

### Exclusive to Fundador / Experto
- #charlas-semanales (live sessions channel)
- #analisis-de-mercado
- #estrategias-y-debates
- #recursos-compartidos

### Optional private channels
- #feedback-y-sugerencias (Fundador/Experto only)

## Live Sessions Guidelines (#charlas-semanales)

- Only accessible to Fundador and Experto roles.
- This is the **main premium experience**: every week I unpack the topics covered in the newsletter, add deeper context, share my current thinking, and answer questions live.
- Sessions are announced 24-48 hours in advance.
- Recording + summary is usually shared afterward for members who couldn't attend.
- Keep a professional, high-signal tone. No pumps, no low-quality shilling.

## Moderation Principles
- Maintain a respectful, serious environment.
- The founder (CBas) should have visible but not constant presence to avoid burnout.
- Use slow mode during live sessions if needed.

## Future Considerations
- When recurring revenue is introduced, annual members should receive the same Discord access as lifetime Fundador/Experto members for the duration of their active period.
